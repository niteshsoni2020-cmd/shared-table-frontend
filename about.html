Why I Started This

There’s a moment that changed how I understood travel. It wasn’t a monument, or a tour, or a museum. It was a dinner table.

I was travelling alone, tired, and unsure. A stranger invited me for a simple home-cooked meal. For two hours, we shared stories, laughter, and silence. I walked away feeling less like a tourist — and more like I belonged.

I realized that food brings people together faster than anything else. So I built The Shared Table Story to help travellers meet locals not through attractions, but through human connection.

Abhishek

Founder, Traveller, Story Collector
