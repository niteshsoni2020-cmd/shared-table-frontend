We believe that sharing a meal or a walk is the best way to understand a new place. We connect curious travelers with local hosts.

Our Story

The Shared Table Story began in Melbourne with a simple idea: why eat alone in a restaurant when you could eat with a local family? What started as a small experiment has grown into a community of hosts across Australia sharing everything from Diwali dinners to Outback treks.

We are not a tour company. We are a marketplace for connection. Our hosts are real people—home cooks, storytellers, and custodians of culture.
