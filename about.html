Why I Started The Shared Table Story

There’s a moment that changed how I understood travel. It wasn’t a monument, or a tour, or a museum. It was a dinner table.

I was travelling, alone, tired, and unsure of what I would find. A stranger invited me for a simple home-cooked meal. For two hours, we shared stories, memories, laughter, and silence. I walked away feeling less like a tourist — and more like I belonged.

That moment stayed with me. I realised something: Food brings people together faster than anything else.

So I built The Shared Table Story for one purpose: To help travellers meet locals not through attractions — but through human connection. Some platforms show you places. This one shows you people.

— Your Fellow Traveler
Founder, Traveller, Story Collector.
