Privacy Policy

Last Updated: December 2025

1. Information We Collect

We collect information you provide directly to us, such as your name, email address, profile picture, and payment information when you make a booking.

2. How We Use Your Data

We use your data to facilitate bookings, process payments, and improve platform security. We do not sell your personal data to third-party advertisers.

3. Third-Party Processors

We trust secure partners to handle specific functions: Stripe for secure payments and Cloudinary for image hosting. Your financial data is never stored on our servers.

4. Cookie Policy

We use cookies to maintain your login session and remember your preferences. You can disable cookies in your browser settings, but some platform features may not work.

5. Contact Us

If you have questions about your data, please contact us at support@thesharedtablestory.com.
